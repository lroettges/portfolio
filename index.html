Laura Roettges

I am a creative generalist with a wide variety of interests. Through this site I aim to display my work and skills, but more importantly to express what makes me - me. I hope future employers want to work with me for who I am not just for the work I do, which is why I seek to share a holistic self depiction.

I have extensive event programming experience and enjoy the social nature of the work, however my biggest current interest is in seeking to connect technology and art in a productive way. Art and technology are much more interrelated than often perceived on the surface level and my interest in data analytics, data visualization, and web design have grown extensively over the past year. I plan to expand my portfolio into these realms in the near future.
