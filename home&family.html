The Sunshine State

Though I currently live in St. Louis, MO while I attend school. My true home is back in Florida. I was born and lived for 10 years in Jacksonville, Florida until my family moved "over the ditch" (aka over the intercostal waterway) to Ponte Vedra Beach which is only about 35 minutes away. I love the beach and ocean and grew up swimming, surfing, fishing, kyaking, boogie-boarding, jr. lifegaurding, etc. Overall living in such an environment has shaped my love for adventure, my go-getter yet laid back attitude, and my apreciation for the environment.
